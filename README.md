# Customize Your Resume

This README will guide you on how to customize the resume with your personal information.

## Instructions

1. **Open `resume.md`**: Locate the `resume.md` file in the repository.
2. **Edit Personal Information**:
   - Replace placeholders like `Your Name`, `Your Address`, etc. with your actual details.

3. **Updating Experience**:
   - In the experience section, list your previous jobs, focusing on the tasks and responsibilities relevant to your career goals.

4. **Modify Education Section**:
   - Include your educational qualifications, achievements, and any relevant certifications.

5. **Summary**:
   - Tailor the summary to reflect your strengths, areas of expertise, and what you bring to potential employers.

6. **Save Changes**: After editing, make sure to save your changes before committing.

7. **Preview**: Use a Markdown viewer to preview how your resume will look after the customization.

## Tips
- Keep the format clean and professional.
- Use bullet points for easy readability.
- Ensure spelling and grammar are correct.
